Biella Linen Wool Polo

A fully fashioned Johnny collar polo inspired by 1960s open-collar styles. Knit in Italy from a refined linen and ultra-soft merino wool blend, the yarn balances crisp coolness with natural warmth and fluid drape. Spun at a historic Italian mill, it’s yarn-dyed for depth of color and garment-rinsed for softness, then shaped with a saddle armhole and finished with ribbed collar, cuffs, and hem.

Vintage fit with a shorter body length, slightly longer sleeves, and ribbed cuffs and hem.

53% linen, 47% merino wool.
